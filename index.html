Teaching Style and Approach

Hayden's approach is structured and personalized, focusing on understanding music fundamentals to move beyond rote patterns. For beginners/intermediates, emphasis is on building a solid foundation; for advanced players, it's coaching on improvisation and techniques. Lessons are goal-oriented, encouraging real-world application and fretboard mastery.
